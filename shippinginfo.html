Shipping Information

We are committed to delivering your orders safely and on time.

Order Processing:
Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.
Delivery Time:
Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days, Express Shipping: 1-3 business days (available in selected locations).
Shipping Charges:
Free shipping on orders above ₹999,A standard shipping fee of ₹99 applies to orders below ₹999.
Delivery Locations:
We deliver to most cities and towns across India,Delivery availability depends on your PIN code.
Order Tracking:
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking ID via email or SMS,You can use the tracking number to monitor your shipment status.
