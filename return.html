Return policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with your order, you may return eligible items by following the policy below.

1.Returns are accepted within 7 days of receiving your order.
2.Products must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.
3.Items that are damaged due to misuse or normal wear and tear are not eligible for return.
4.Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund or replacement.
5.Refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after approval.
6.Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product received is damaged or incorrect.
7.If you receive a defective, damaged, or wrong product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item.
